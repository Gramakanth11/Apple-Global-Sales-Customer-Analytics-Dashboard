# Apple-Global-Sales-Customer-Analytics-Dashboard

##  Project Overview
Built an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze Apple’s global sales data, focusing on revenue trends, product performance, customer behavior, and return patterns. The project includes KPI tracking, time-series analysis, geographic insights, and customer satisfaction analysis to support data-driven business decisions.

---

##  Objectives
- Analyze global revenue and unit sales trends over time
- Identify top-performing products, categories, and regions
- Understand customer behavior through ratings, returns, and payment methods
- Evaluate the relationship between customer satisfaction and product returns

---

##  Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- DAX
- CSV / Excel Dataset

##  Key KPIs
- **Total Revenue (USD)**
- **Units Sold**
- **Total Transactions**
- **Average Selling Price (ASP)**
- **Return Rate (%)**

---

##  Dashboard Features
- Monthly revenue and units sold trend analysis
- City-level geographic revenue distribution
- Product category and top product performance analysis
- Payment method distribution across sales channels
- Customer rating distribution and return behavior analysis
- Interactive slicers for year, country, category, and sales channel

---

##  Key Insights
- Revenue shows strong seasonality, with noticeable peaks in specific periods
- Premium product categories contribute higher revenue despite lower unit volume
- Urban and developed markets drive a significant share of global revenue
- Lower customer ratings are associated with higher return rates
- Digital payment methods dominate online sales channels
